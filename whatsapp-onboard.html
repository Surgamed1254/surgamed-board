WhatsApp Coexistence onboarding

Internal admin tool · SurgaMed CRM production app (App ID 1231523598981623)

Connects a rep's existing WhatsApp Business app number to the Cloud API in Coexistence mode — the number stays live on the phone (no migration, chat history preserved). The same flow works for every rep; nothing here is hardcoded to one person or number.

How to onboard a rep:

  1. The rep's phone has the latest WhatsApp Business app and the number is not on any other WABA.
  2. Click Connect a WhatsApp Business App below.
  3. In the Meta popup, choose “Connect a WhatsApp Business App” (the Coexistence option — never a "migrate" / "move number" option).
  4. Scan the QR shown, on the phone, from WhatsApp Business → Settings → Linked devices → Link a device.
  5. On success, the issued Phone Number ID and WABA ID appear below — copy them for the rep's mapping.

✅ Connected — copy these for the rep mapping

Map the Phone Number ID to the rep's reps.phone_number_id. Do not enable ingestion until mapping is confirmed.
