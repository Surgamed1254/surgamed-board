SurgaMed CRM Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

SurgaMed ("we", "us") operates the SurgaMed CRM, an internal business application used by our own sales team to manage customer communications, including WhatsApp Business messages, email, orders and shipping information. This policy explains what data the application processes and how.

Information we collect

  • Business contact information: names, phone numbers, email addresses and company details of customers who communicate with our sales team.
  • Message content and metadata from conversations customers have with our sales representatives on WhatsApp Business, email and other business channels.
  • Order, payment status and shipping information related to customer purchases.

How we use information

  • To respond to customer inquiries and manage sales conversations.
  • To track orders, quotes and shipments.
  • To improve our internal sales processes.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use it for third-party advertising.

How information is shared

Data is processed by service providers acting on our behalf: Meta Platforms (WhatsApp Business Platform), Supabase (database hosting), Microsoft (email), and Slack (internal notifications limited to event metadata, never message content). Access within SurgaMed is limited to authorized staff.

Data retention

We retain business communications and transaction records for as long as needed for legitimate business and legal purposes, after which they are deleted.

Data deletion requests

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time. Email orders@surgamed.com with the subject "Data deletion request" and include the phone number or email address associated with your conversations. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit, stored with access controls, and webhook payloads are cryptographically verified.

Children

Our services are for business customers and are not directed to children under 18.

Changes

We may update this policy; the latest version will always be available at this address.
